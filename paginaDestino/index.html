Xiaomi Mi 11


High performance

The model is powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 888 SoC. Thanks to its 5nm process technology, a powerful Cortex X1 processor (2.84 GHz), and an Adreno 660 GPU (840 MHz), the device offers high performance. There is LPDDR5 RAM with a speed of 6400 Mbps. The device supports 5G network and Wi-Fi 6E.

Powerful battery

The flagship is equipped with a powerful 4600 mAh battery. It provides a long battery life and fast charging (up to 45 minutes for full charge). The smartphone also supports wireless and reverse charging.

Display with perfect graphics

The 6.81-inch AMOLED display is protected with Corning Gorilla Glass. The maximum brightness value reaches 1500 nits. The flagship screen supports a 2K resolution. The refresh rate is 120 Hz.
